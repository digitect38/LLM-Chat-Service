Predictive Diagnostics
3-Tier Hybrid Knowledge Engine

No single knowledge source dependency. Fuses three knowledge tiers — data, experience, and documents — to deliver diagnostics that work from day one and grow more accurate over time.

Tier 1

Data-Driven

ML models that learn automatically from sensor data. Detect multivariate patterns at scale that humans miss.

Models: Isolation Forest, AutoEncoder, LSTM
Output: RUL prediction, failure probability, degradation trends
Strength: Unbiased, massively scalable
Limitation: Cold start 30+ days, hard to interpret
Tier 2

Expert Knowledge

Conditional causal knowledge accumulated by field engineers. Knowledge that only comes from experience, not manuals.

Input: Structured templates + conversation mining + action history
Validation: Automated post-hoc verification against Tier 1 data
Scope: Per-model (default) or per-instance
Strength: Conditional causality, reduced false alarms
Tier 3

Document-Grounded

Diagnostic knowledge auto-extracted from manuals and standards. Works instantly from day one with zero cold start.

Extraction: Troubleshooting sections, error codes, PM procedures
Standards: Auto-extraction from SEMI · IEC · ISO industry standards
Strength: Day 1 ready, based on official procedures
Limitation: No instance-specific adaptation, documented scope only
⚡

Fusion Engine — Multi-Ranked Diagnostics

Combines evidence from all three tiers with weighted fusion to produce ranked diagnostic results. Each diagnosis includes confidence scores, source tier attribution, and manual citations — a complete evidence package for the engineer's final judgment.
